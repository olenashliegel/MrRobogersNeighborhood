Welcome to Mr. Roboger's Neighborhood

This interactive app transforms numbers into fun phrases. Simply enter a number into the field below, and the app will generate a list of all numbers from 0 up to your input, with the following whimsical substitutions:

  • Numbers containing the digit 1 are transformed into "Beep!"
  • Numbers containing the digit 2 become "Boop!"
  • Numbers containing the digit 3 are turned into "Won't you be my neighbor?"

Explore and enjoy the playful transformations crafted by Mr. Roboger!
